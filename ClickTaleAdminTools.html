Enabling/Disabling ClickTale for this user/domain.

This is part of ClickTale's utilities for web owners.

ClickTale uses cookies to differentiate visitors and maintain their states.

Force yourself to be recorded on the current domain.

Block yourself from being recorded on the current domain.

Reset the state so that ClickTale will be forced to re-classify the user on his next recorded session.

You might need to do this separately for each browser and domain that you wish to use.

For more information, visit our beta forum.
